Liana Lianov, MD, MPH, FACLM, FACPM, DipABLM — Bio

Dr. Lianov has shared her expertise on various platforms and through scholarly articles and books, recently including Roots of Positive Change, the Routledge International Handbook of Positive Health Sciences, and Lifestyle Medicine From the Inside Out.

Tracing Our Footsteps

About the Toolkit

For over a decade, Dr. Liana Lianov, a physician leader in lifestyle medicine, positive psychology, and positive health, has advanced and expanded these fields in diverse health care settings. In 2020, she founded the Global Positive Health Institute to promote the wellbeing of health professionals and their patients. During this same period, Elena Lucciarini, PhD candidate and graduate of a Master’s in Applied Positive Psychology, conducted an extensive review of effective positive psychology interventions. She examined the original research to extract precise instructions and action steps used in evidence-based studies. In collaboration with Dr. Ilona Boniwell—a pioneer in the positive psychology movement, Associate Professor at HEC Paris, and CEO of Positran—these interventions were adapted for educational settings. For nearly three decades, Dr. Boniwell has been teaching, consulting, and applying positive psychology practices across the globe. This unique collaboration between Drs. Lianov, Lucciarini, and Boniwell led to the creation of MicrodosePlus. With guidance from health care advisors, Dr. Lianov further refined the interventions for clinical and health professional settings, packaging them into an accessible, user-friendly digital toolkit. Unlike AI-generated content, this toolkit offers only evidence-based practices, complete with original research references and step-by-step instructions grounded in science. Our commitment is to remain true to what research has proven effective—while continuing to evolve with your feedback.
